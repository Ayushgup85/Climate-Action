Climate Action

Info on climate change

Climate change includes global warming caused by human emissions and the resulting large scale weather changes. These changes can negatively affect our lives and our planet. Below are some sources to educate you further.

Actions to prevent climate change

There are several solutions to make progress against climate change. These include using renewable energy rather than sources that produce high emissions, only buying food that you know you will eat, reducing your water waste, and more. You can also stay informed on climate change and donate to trustworthy organizations. To gain a better understanding, check out the websites below.
